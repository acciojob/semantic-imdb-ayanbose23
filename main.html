Summary

A skilled thief who steals corporate secrets through use of dream-sharing technology is given the inverse task of planting an idea into the mind of a C.E.O. Assembling a team, he must navigate layered dream worlds and his own troubled subconscious to pull off the perfect inception.

Actors

  • Arthur — Joseph Gordon-Levitt (Cobb's right-hand, the team's point of stability)
  • Ariadne — Ellen Page (the architect who designs the dreamscapes)
  • Robert Fischer — Cillian Murphy (the mark whose mind the team targets)
