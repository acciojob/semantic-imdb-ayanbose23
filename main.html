Summary

"Inception" is a science fiction thriller that explores the dream world. Dom Cobb, a skilled thief, enters people's dreams to extract secrets and is tasked with planting an idea in a target's subconscious.

Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio stars as Dom Cobb. Other notable actors include Joseph Gordon-Levitt and Ellen Page.
